Global Beauty Industry Revenue

An in-depth look at global beauty industry trends, revenue from top manufacturers, sales channels, and consumer demographics.

In 2023, the US beauty industry reached 98 billion USD, a rise from 86 billion in 2018, showing steady growth. China followed with 67 billion USD, up from 55 billion, driven by increasing online sales. Japan's beauty revenue saw slower growth, from 41 billion USD in 2018 to 47 billion in 2023, indicating a more stable market compared to China.
